The SoP Media Group is for those of you that are interested in journalism, being it the latest pharmaceutical news and articles, or local news pertaining to the SoP Community.

Joining this club will enable you to develop your writing and communication skills, and to be the forefront of very important research going on right under our noses in SoP. If you are interested, you will taught how to code and manage the Students' Union website, a skill future employers will admire!


Items to be Covered


The latest pharmaceutical and healthcare news

Research and advances made in healthcare from SoP

Capturing individual profiles from SoP ("Humans of SoP")

Capturing the progress of the clubs and societies in SoP

General upkeep of the SU website
