SASO?




The Student and Academic Support Office may seem like just a place to hand in the countless coursework assignments and essays, but it is more than meets the eye.


SASO is there to deal with student queries about various matters relating to their academic life at the School of Pharmacy. Some of the advice and support given include:


  • Advice on admission to the programmes at SoP
  • Advice on programmes and modules
  • Advice on university regulations
  • Coursework hand in and collection
  • Exams and feedback
  • Fitness to Practice (MPharm)
  • Help and Support
  • Hospital and community placements
  • Postgraduate funding
  • Student Ambassadors
  • Study Abroad placements
