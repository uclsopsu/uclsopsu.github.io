The music club just seeks to bring together musicians in the School of Pharmacy, both lecturers and students! We will have regular rehearsals/jam sessions together, as well as perform in in-house events (such as alumni dinners etc). SoP historically had a talent show every February, which the Student Union is trying to relive, with the musicians making a timely appearance in the show.

The music club will also perform voluntarily for charities such as the Cancer Research UK and the Great Ormond Street Hospital, especially during the Christmas period where we will record and perform for these charities.

Join us to be part of this incredibly fun and entertaining club, no matter your music (instrument or vocal) skillset. We can accommodate anyone and everyone!


Rehearsal Sessions

Our rehearsal sessions will normally take place in the Mapplethorpe Lecture Theatre (MLT) on the ground floor of the School of Phamracy.
