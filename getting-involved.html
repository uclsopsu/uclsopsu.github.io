Men's Football Club




After a lot of commitment, dedication and keenness, the School of Pharmacy's Men's FC has been resurrected from the sunken place! Join us to be part of the first School of Pharmacy Football team to compete in the London Universities Sports League (LUSL). Whether you are the occasional football player looking to up your fitness game, or you are the next Ronald or Messi, you are welcome to join us in our training sessions and/or games. The team ranges from First Years to PhDs, with the formidable UCLU Rugby First Team Captain Kayode Awobowale making sure we are hypoxic on monday evenings. The team also has a lot of socials, making us team mates on and off the pitch.



Training Sessions

Our training sessions take place on Monday evenings, from 19:00 to 21:00 at Coram Fields (just opposite the School of Pharmacy). You can join us anytime!

Women's Football Club




If we can be sure of one thing last season, it was that the SoP Women's team stood toe-to-toe with the Men's FC in fitness training sessions, and that they beat the Men's team on attendance. The SoP Women's team is a place that offers a break from the degree, providing at atmosphere of pure joy and team spirit. This Girl Can, and yes we are beating the stereotypes!! Join these great young women and become part of something historical, alongside out-lasting the Men on the fitness pitch.



Training Sessions

Our training sessions take place on Monday evenings, from 19:00 to 21:00 at Coram Fields (just opposite the School of Pharmacy). You can join us anytime!
